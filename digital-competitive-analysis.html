Who Should You Analyze as Competitors in Digital Marketing?

Competitor analysis normally includes your direct and indirect competitors — those providing the same (or similar) solutions to the same (or similar) audiences. Online competitor analysis should cover them, plus one more group of rivals: your brand competitors that fight for the same users you do. They don’t necessarily need to sell them a similar product or service, but they definitely want to get their attention.
